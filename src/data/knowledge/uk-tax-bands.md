# UK Income Tax Bands 2025/26

The UK income tax system uses progressive tax bands. Everyone gets a tax-free Personal Allowance of £12,570 per year.

## Tax Bands

- **Personal Allowance:** £0 – £12,570 → 0% tax
- **Basic Rate:** £12,571 – £50,270 → 20% tax
- **Higher Rate:** £50,271 – £125,140 → 40% tax
- **Additional Rate:** Over £125,140 → 45% tax

## Key Rules

- The Personal Allowance reduces by £1 for every £2 earned above £100,000 (fully lost at £125,140).
- National Insurance: employees pay 8% on earnings between £12,570 and £50,270, then 2% above that.
- Marriage Allowance lets you transfer £1,260 of unused Personal Allowance to a spouse (saving up to £252/year).
- Self-employed pay Class 2 NI (flat rate) and Class 4 NI (6% on profits £12,570–£50,270, 2% above).
- Tax year runs from 6 April to 5 April the following year.
- Self-assessment deadline: 31 January (online) for the previous tax year.
