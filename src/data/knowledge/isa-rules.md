# UK ISA Rules 2025/26

Individual Savings Accounts (ISAs) let you save and invest tax-free. No income tax on interest, no capital gains tax on growth.

## Annual Allowance

- **Total ISA allowance:** £20,000 per tax year across all ISA types.
- You can split the allowance between different ISA types however you like.

## ISA Types

- **Cash ISA:** Tax-free savings account. Available from age 18.
- **Stocks & Shares ISA:** Invest in funds, shares, bonds tax-free. Available from age 18.
- **Innovative Finance ISA (IFISA):** Peer-to-peer lending, tax-free. Available from age 18.
- **Lifetime ISA (LISA):** For first home or retirement. 25% government bonus on contributions up to £4,000/year. Must be opened before age 40. Withdrawal penalty of 25% on early access (unless buying first home or aged 60+).
- **Junior ISA:** For under-18s. £9,000 annual limit. Child cannot access until 18.

## Key Rules

- Use it or lose it — unused allowance does not carry over.
- You can transfer between ISA providers without affecting your allowance.
- Flexible ISAs let you withdraw and replace money in the same tax year without using extra allowance.
- You can open one of each ISA type per tax year.
